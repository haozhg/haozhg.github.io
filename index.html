About me

I am a research scientist at Bloomberg LP AI Group. My research interest include data-driven modeling, model reduction, fluid dynamics, dynamical system, control theory, machine learning, and natural language processing. I am interested in discussing interesting ideas and pursuing new collaborations.

I received M.A and Ph.D. in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering from Princeton University in 2020, under the supervision of Prof. Clancy Rowley. My PhD thesis is on data-driven modeling for fluid dynamics and control. I obtained my B.Eng. degree in Engineering Mechanics and B.A. degree in Economics from Peking University in 2014.
